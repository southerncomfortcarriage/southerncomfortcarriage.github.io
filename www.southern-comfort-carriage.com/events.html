Weddings

Nothing is more romantic than a horse-drawn carriage ride for your wedding. We can deliver the bride to the ceremony (at a garden, park, church, or private location). We can wait outside the church and at the end of the ceremony transport bride and groom to nearby reception, short ride, hotel, or waiting limousine.


Birthdays

Let your little princess be Cinderella for a day. On her birthday or other special day give her a ride in a horse-drawn carriage. A picnic in the park, at your home, or other location perfect for children. Our drivers dress in tuxedos.

For little boys birthdays the drivers dress up like cowboys. Invite their friends, throw a party.
