Experience the luxury and southern charm of a horse-drawn carriage tour through historic downtown Nashville. All the thrill and excitement of yesteryear come to life as you leisurely enjoy some of Music City's most significant landmarks.

Add a touch of elegance to your afternoon or evening and carriage from your hotel to your dining destination. After dinner, take a romantic ride and see a beautiful view of the city, the famous Ryman Auditorium (original home of the Grand Ole Opry), Printers Alley, Fort Nashboro, the Country Music Hall of Fame, Tootsie's Orchid Lounge, or Ernest Tubbs Record Shop.

Southern Comfort Carriage Service engages knowledgeable and courteous drivers, the finest groomed horses and luxurious carriages to further enhance your special occasion.


Carriages are located on Broadway at the corner of Third Avenue
